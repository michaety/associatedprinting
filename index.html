About Us

Associated Printing Group Pty Ltd is a local screen printer located at 8 Rowood Rd, Prospect NSW 2148. We are a locally owned & operated screen printer specialist providing a wide range of services from custom labelling to cloth labelling. We pride ourselves in providing professional & quality services throughout Prospect & surrounding areas. We only use quality products. Call us today for a free quote!

Established in 1990, Associated Printing Group has become synonymous with quality printing jobs. We are 100% Australian-owned and operate locally across all areas in Prospect and its surroundings. We are registered and licensed to undertake all types of printing works. Equipped with more than 30 years of experience, we provide the whole range of printing services.
